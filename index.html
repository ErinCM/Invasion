About Us

Invasion is an app that allows for our users to track and post sightings of UFOs from anywhere in the world. Have you ever seen a UFO and wish that there was an easy way to share this amazing event with others? Now you can with our easy to use app! Our app allows you to see all the sightings near you as soon as someone posts. You can then comment on or save these sightings to view later. Invasion also provides an easy to use map so you can view and track sightings all over the world. For both enthusiasts and new comers alike, Invasion provides a fun and easy way for users to share their close encounters!

Why Choose Invasion?

Invasion is a unique and easy to use app that is one of a kind. As of our release there is no other app that provides an easy to use system for tracking and posting UFO sightings. Although there are websites that allow for this functionality none of them provide the easy to use platform that Invasion provides. Our goal is to give our users simple functionality without being overwhelmed with too much information. Invasion goes to the core of what users want out of an easy to use tracking system while at the same time also providing the ability to share their encounters. Therefore this allows for the spread of the UFO community and and more widespread interest in UFO tracking.
